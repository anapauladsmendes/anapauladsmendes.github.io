I’m a Computer Science student at Universidade Federal do Piauí. I have technical background in Software Development at Instituto Federal de Ciência e Tecnologia do Piauí. I’m currently working as Full Stack Developer at Quiploy. I was a volunteer Software Developer Technical Lead in the open source project, Colaboradados. In academic life I’m a researcher at the Artificial Intelligence Laboratory, LINA, in the Computing department of the Universidade Federal do Piauí.

I co-founded PyLadies Teresina and Meninas Digitais Piauí. I am currently active in Meninas Digitais Piauí. As a member of PyLadies Brazil, I love helping women's communities in technology whenever I can.

Besides these roles, I dedicate a portion of my time on a personal project called Ana no Terminal where I share knowledge about code, science and technology. The focus of the project is to help people interested in computer science with content entirely in Portuguese, as there is still a shortage of computer content in this language.

I have a huge passion for teaching and sharing knowledge as much as I can. I believe that information in the right hands has the power to move the world and offer better conditions for society.

My interest and love for computing has been alive since I was 10 years old. I developed a study routine and I'm seeking more knowledge through projects. Currently the areas I am most involved are web development and artificial intelligence.

I'm in love with open source, communities and the power of women in technology. If you want to chat a little more with me, you can contact me through my social networks.
